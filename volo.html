Volo
ICON DESIGN
OVERVIEW

Volo is an icon theme for retina iPhone and iPad devices, availble on the Cydia store. Volo includes over 300 hand crafted icons, in two different styles. UI elements across the system have also been themed, such as in Control Center and the Settings app. Volo has recieved over 15,000 cumulative downloads (some more legal than others).

MY ROLE

Since the begining, Volo has been very important to me. It was the first major product that I had shipped to the public, and I wanted it to be the best that it could. I spent many hours taking user requests for additional icons, and creating multiple styles and variations until I could finally decide on the final design. All icons were created as vectors, resulting in an easy adaptation to each screen size, and streamlining future updates when new devices are released.
