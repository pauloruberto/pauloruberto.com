OVERVIEW

This website was created for St. Micheals College at the University of Toronto. All of the information regarding the yealy student orienation was present. Details on registration, scheduling, and more were all present. Each of the incoming students visited this webpage over the course of the week to gain all of this information. Take a look at the completed project!

MY ROLE

I was contacted and asked to design and develop the website, while working alongside the orientation co-ordinators. As the project developed, and the requirements evolved, I worked closely with the co-ordinators to meet their needs and desires.
